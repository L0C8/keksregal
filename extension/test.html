keksregal Test Page

How to test the extension:

  1. Load the extension in Chrome (see CHROME_EXTENSION.md)
  2. Visit any website with cookies (like google.com, youtube.com, etc.)
  3. Click the keksregal icon in your toolbar
  4. Click "Scan Current Site" or "Scan All Cookies"
  5. View the detailed report!
